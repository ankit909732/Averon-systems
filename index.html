Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

The answers you need before starting a conversation with us.

What does a typical project cost?
Projects typically range from $15k for a focused Salesforce customisation to $80k+ for a full-stack platform build. We scope every project individually after a discovery call — no generic pricing tiers.
How long does a project take?
Most projects take 2–5 months depending on scope. We always define a clear timeline in the architecture phase so there are no surprises. Two-week sprint cycles keep everything predictable.
Do you work with startups or only enterprises?
Both. We've helped seed-stage startups build their first product and helped large enterprises modernise legacy systems. The common thread is teams that value quality engineering over just "getting something done."
What's your tech stack?
React.js and Next.js on the frontend, Node.js and Python (Django/FastAPI) on the backend, PostgreSQL for databases, and the full Salesforce platform (Apex, LWC, Flows). We pick the right tool for each project.
Do you provide post-launch support?
Absolutely. We offer ongoing managed support packages — bug fixes, feature additions, performance monitoring, and Salesforce admin support. Most of our clients stay with us long-term.
